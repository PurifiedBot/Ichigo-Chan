Info Commands

k!ping | Check the bots latency (is it alive?)
k!whois | Check a users info!
             --- [Ex: k!whois @User#1234]
k!botinfo | Check the bots general stats!
k!uptime | How long has the bot been online?
k!serverinfo | Check info on the server :)

Utility Commands

k!serverinvite | Get an invite for the channel you want. (idk its just fun. Uses channel ID)
                    --- [Ex: k!serverinvite 730625556747649115]

Music Commands

k!play | Play music from youtube!
           --- [Ex: k!play https://youtu.be/QLdAhwSBZ3w]
k!queue | Shows the servers music queue
k!np | Shows the current playing song!
k!stop | Stop the player
k!pause | Pause the player
k!resume | Resume the player
k!skip | Skip the current song
k!loop | Loop the current song
k!volume | Change the volume (1-100)
